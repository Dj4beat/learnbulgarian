Vocabulary
Revision

Vocabulary is the fuel of fluency. Today you systematically review all core word groups from the course — identifying gaps, strengthening weak areas, and consolidating the words that will serve you in the oral and written exams.

📅 Day 54⏳ ~60 minutes🎤 Audio on every section✅ Quiz at the end
🔊 Speed:

💡 How to use this vocabulary revision

For each section: (1) cover the English column, (2) say the Bulgarian meaning aloud, (3) tick words you know confidently. (4) mark uncertain ones with a star — come back to them. The 8-question quiz at the end tests random words from across all sections.

🎯 Why this revision matters

Vocabulary revision is where fluency becomes practical. These are the words that help with shops, doctors, offices, landlords, transport, family life, and everyday opinion-sharing in Bulgaria.

This page should not feel like a dead glossary. It should help learners recognise the high-value words fast and hear them often enough to use them confidently.

Section 10

Writing task — vocabulary self-test

✏️ Writing task — Day 54

  1. Go through sections 1–4 and write one original sentence for each word group (8 sentences total).
  2. Choose 10 words from today that you found difficult and write them in sentences.
  3. Translate without looking: (a) I'm looking for a two-room flat to rent. (b) I have a bachelor's degree in economics. (c) According to me, it's getting more and more expensive.
  4. Write 5 sentences using idioms from Section 9.
