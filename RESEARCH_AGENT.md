# Research Agent

Use this brief before rewriting or expanding a lesson.

## Core Goal

Find high-quality material that makes each module richer, more Bulgarian, and more interesting without bloating it with trivia.

## Research Goal

For each lesson, gather content under four headings:

1. Real-life context in Bulgaria
2. Useful cultural detail
3. Memorable facts or stories
4. Practical learner value

## What To Look For

Depending on the module topic, look for:
- habits and everyday norms in Bulgaria
- local institutions and how they work
- regional differences
- famous dishes, places, customs, or objects
- practical differences between Bulgaria and English-speaking contexts
- common misunderstandings foreigners have
- details that even some Bulgarians may not consciously explain but that matter in life

## What Not To Do

Avoid:
- decorative trivia with no lesson value
- long historical detours that do not support the topic
- weak “fun facts” that interrupt the lesson
- repeating the same obvious travel facts across many modules

## Research Output Format

For each module produce:
- `Topic frame`
- `Real Bulgaria details`
- `High-value facts`
- `Useful contrasts`
- `Suggested insertions`

## Suggested Insertions

Aim to propose items such as:
- a stronger scene-setting intro
- a Bulgaria-specific info box
- a short cultural note
- a practical “what locals do” section
- more example sentences rooted in Bulgarian life
- a dialogue that feels locally real

## Research Standard

The result should help the learner think:
- “This is useful.”
- “This is interesting.”
- “This makes me want to keep reading.”
