🌍 Setting the scene

Bulgaria is not just a hotel-country for summer tourists. It is a country of Black Sea resorts, old towns, spa destinations with mineral water, ski areas, mountain huts, family guesthouses, and weekend city breaks. If you understand accommodation vocabulary, you understand a surprisingly large part of how modern Bulgaria moves.

According to Bulgaria's National Statistical Institute, in July 2025 the country recorded about 2.0 million visits by non-residents, while in the fourth quarter of 2024 most Bulgarian residents who made tourist trips travelled only inside Bulgaria. That combination matters: Bulgaria is both a visitor destination and a country whose own people travel actively around it.

So this lesson is not only about asking for a room key. It is also about understanding why Bulgarians talk about the seaside, mountain huts, parking in old centres, family hotels, and spa weekends with the same ease that other countries talk about chain hotels and airports.

Section 5

Bulgarian stay culture and local detail

Useful things many visitors do not know before they arrive.

Accommodation in Bulgaria tells you a lot about the country itself. In Sofia, Plovdiv, and Varna, you will see everything from modern city hotels to small family guesthouses. On the Black Sea coast, стаи под наем are still a very Bulgarian experience: simple family-run rooms, often cheaper than large hotels, and usually much more personal.

🌞 Seaside Bulgaria

Along the coast, many Bulgarians still prefer smaller family accommodation over anonymous resort blocks. If you stay in стаи под наем, expect a more direct human style: the owner may greet you personally, show you the room, and tell you where to eat, park, or avoid traffic.

🎤 Bulgarian stay culture

💡 Bulgaria detail that surprises visitors

Many foreigners discover Bulgaria first through beaches or ski resorts, but Bulgarians themselves often value weekend stays in mountain towns, monastery areas, old revival towns, and spa regions with mineral water. If you know words like къща за гости, стар град, and СПА хотел, you suddenly understand a much more local Bulgaria.

Section 7

Dialogue — checking into a hotel

A complete hotel check-in conversation.

💬 Dialogue — hotel check-inRead aloud
Reception

Добър вечер! Добре дошли.

Dobar vecher! Dobre doshli.

Good evening. Welcome.

Guest

Добър вечер. Имам резервация на името Смит.

Dobar vecher. Imam rezervatsiya na imeto Smit.

Good evening. I have a reservation under the name Smith.

Reception

Момент, моля. Да, г-н Смит. Двойна стая за три нощи.

Moment, molya. Da, g-n Smit. Dvoyna staya za tri noshti.

One moment, please. Yes, Mr Smith. A double room for three nights.

Guest

Точно така. Включена ли е закуската?

Tochno taka. Vklyuchena li e zakuskata?

That is right. Is breakfast included?

Reception

Да, закуската е включена. От седем до десет сутринта в ресторанта.

Da, zakuskata e vklyuchena. Ot sedem do deset sutrinta v restoranta.

Yes, breakfast is included. From seven to ten in the morning in the restaurant.

Guest

Перфектно. Имате ли паркинг?

Perfektno. Imate li parking?

Perfect. Do you have parking?

Reception

Да, има безплатен паркинг зад хотела. Ето ключа ви — стая двеста и осем, на втория етаж.

Da, ima bezplaten parking zad hotela. Eto klyucha vi — staya dvesta i osem, na vtoriya etazh.

Yes, there is free parking behind the hotel. Here is your key: room 208, on the second floor.

Guest

Благодаря. Каква е паролата за Wi-Fi?

Blagodarya. Kakva e parolata za Wi-Fi?

Thank you. What is the Wi-Fi password?

Reception

Паролата е на картата в стаята. Ако имате нужда от нещо, обадете се на рецепцията.

Parolata e na kartata v stayata. Ako imate nuzhda ot neshto, obadete se na retseptsiyata.

The password is on the card in the room. If you need anything, call reception.

Writing task

✏️ Writing task — Day 28

  1. Write a hotel booking enquiry: you want a double room for 4 nights with breakfast, and need to know the price.
  2. Translate: "The shower is not working and the room is very noisy. Could you give me a different room?"
  3. What is the difference between хотел, квартира, къща за гости and хижа?
  4. Write check-in phrases for: reservation name, number of nights, check-in time, Wi-Fi password request.
  5. You are going to Plovdiv for a weekend. Write 4-5 sentences about the kind of accommodation you want and why.
Show answers

1. E.g. Искам да направя резервация. Двойна стая за четири нощи с включена закуска. Колко струва на нощ?

2. Душът не работи и стаята е много шумна. Можете ли да ми дадете друга стая?

3. хотел = commercial hotel. квартира = rented flat/apartment. къща за гости = guesthouse, often smaller and more personal. хижа = mountain hut for hikers.

4. Имам резервация на името... · За X нощи. · В колко часа е настаняването? · Каква е паролата за Wi-Fi?

5. E.g. Искам малък семеен хотел близо до стария град. Искам стая с баня и добра закуска. Ако има паркомясто, още по-добре.

💡 Tip: Writing by hand in Cyrillic is the fastest way to lock in new vocabulary. Even five minutes of handwriting beats reading the same words ten times.
