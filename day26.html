Section 2

Describing the weather — key constructions

How to say what the weather is like right now and what it will be.

Bulgarian weather expressions mostly use impersonal constructions — there is no grammatical subject. The most common patterns are вали (it is raining/snowing), духа (the wind is blowing), and adjective + е (it is hot/cold/sunny).

BulgarianRomanisedEnglishAudio
Вали дъжд.Vali dazhd.It is raining.
Вали сняг.Vali snyag.It is snowing.
Духа вятър.Duha vyatar.It is windy.
Гърми.Garmi.It is thundering.
Гърми и святка.Garmi i svyatka.There is thunder and lightning.
Слънчево е.Slanchevo e.It is sunny.
Облачно е.Oblachno e.It is cloudy.
Мъгливо е.Maglivo e.It is foggy.
Горещо е.Goreshto e.It is hot.
Студено е.Studeno e.It is cold.
Хладно е.Hladno e.It is cool.
Топло е.Toplo e.It is warm.
Колко градуса е?Kolko gradusa e?How many degrees is it?
Тридесет градуса.Trideset gradusa.Thirty degrees.
Минус пет градуса.Minus pet gradusa.Minus five degrees.
🎤 Weather expressions

💡 Вали — one verb for rain and snow

Вали covers both rain and snow — what follows it tells you which: вали дъжд (it rains) · вали сняг (it snows). Used alone, вали usually implies rain from context. The past: валя (it rained) — aorist of Group 2. The future: ще вали (it will rain).

Section 4

Weather forecasts and plans

Using weather vocabulary with all three tenses.

BulgarianRomanisedEnglishAudio
Какво е времето днес?Kakvo e vremeto dnes?What is the weather like today?
Каква е прогнозата за утре?Kakva e prognozata za utre?What is the forecast for tomorrow?
Утре ще вали.Utre shte vali.It will rain tomorrow.
Ако е слънчево, ще отидем на плажа.Ako e slanchevo, shte otidem na plazha.If it is sunny, we will go to the beach.
Взимам чадър, защото вали.Vzimam chadar, zashtoto vali.I am taking an umbrella because it is raining.
Обличам се топло.Oblicham se toplo.I am dressing warmly.
Много студено беше вчера.Mnogo studeno beshe vchera.It was very cold yesterday.

Section 5

Useful weather chunks

Short phrases people use constantly in real conversation.

Навън е ужасно студено.Navan e uzhasno studeno.It is terribly cold outside.
Ще взема чадър за всеки случай.Shte vzema chadar za vseki sluchay.I will take an umbrella just in case.
Днес има силен вятър.Dnes ima silen vyatar.There is a strong wind today.
Прогнозата не е много добра.Prognozata ne e mnogo dobra.The forecast is not very good.
Ако е топло, ще излезем.Ako e toplo, shte izlezem.If it is warm, we will go out.

Section 6

Dialogue — talking about the weather

A natural conversation about weather and plans.

Нина

Страшно студено е днес, нали?

Strashno studeno e dnes, nali?

It is terribly cold today, isn't it?

Петър

Да, минус осем градуса тази сутрин. Вали сняг от снощи.

Da, minus osem gradusa tazi sutrin. Vali snyag ot snoshti.

Yes, minus eight degrees this morning. It has been snowing since last night.

Нина

Ти обичаш ли снега?

Ti obichash li snega?

Do you like the snow?

Петър

Обичам го, но не когато трябва да отивам на работа.

Obicham go, no ne kogato tryabva da otivam na rabota.

I like it, but not when I have to go to work.

Нина

Какво ще правиш през уикенда?

Kakvo shte pravish prez uikenda?

What will you do at the weekend?

Петър

Ако продължи да вали, ще остана вкъщи. Ако спре, може би ще ходим на ски в Банско.

Ako prodalzhi da vali, shte ostana vkashti. Ako spre, mozhe bi shte hodim na ski v Bansko.

If it keeps snowing, I will stay at home. If it stops, maybe we will go skiing in Bansko.

Section 7

Writing task

✏️ Writing task — Day 26

  1. Describe today's weather in Bulgarian using at least 5 weather expressions.
  2. Write what the weather is typically like in your country in summer and winter.
  3. Translate: "Yesterday it was sunny and warm. Today it is cloudy and cold. Tomorrow it will snow."
  4. Write two conditional sentences about weather and plans using ако.
Show answers

1. Free answer — use вали, духа, слънчево е, студено е, температурата е X градуса.

2. Free answer — use през лятото / през зимата + weather descriptions.

3. Вчера беше слънчево и топло. Днес е облачно и студено. Утре ще вали сняг.

4. E.g. Ако е горещо, ще отида на плажа. Ако вали, ще остана вкъщи.

💡 Tip: Writing by hand in Cyrillic is the fastest way to lock in new vocabulary. Even five minutes of handwriting beats reading the same words ten times.
